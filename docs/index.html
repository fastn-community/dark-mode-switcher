Overview

dark-mode-switcher-float is used for easily switching from light to dark mode. To use dark-mode-switcher-float on your fastn package, add below into lines FASTN.ftd file:
-- fastn.dependency: fastn-community.github.io/dark-mode-switcher
Lang:
ftd

How to use?

-- import: fastn-community.github.io/dark-mode-switcher as dms

-- dms.dark-mode-switcher-float:
Lang:
ftd

Properties:

This component does not require passing attributes
